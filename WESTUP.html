Mathura, Vrindavan, Barsana & Agra Tour

Duration: 03 Nights / 04 Days

Day 01: Arrival & Vrindavan Sightseeing

Pickup from Mathura and transfer to your hotel. After check-in and refreshing, visit Banke Bihari Ji Temple, Radha Vallabh Ji Temple, Vaishno Devi Temple, Radha Raman Temple, and ISKCON Temple. In the evening, enjoy the Light and Sound Show at Prem Mandir. Overnight stay at Mathura Hotel.

Day 02: Mathura & Gokul Exploration

After breakfast, check out and visit Shri Krishna Janmabhoomi and Dwarkadhish Temple. Proceed to Gokul (15 km from Mathura) and check in at your hotel. After lunch, explore Gokulnath Temple, Raja Thakur Temple, Brahmand Ghat, Raman Reti, Chintaharan Mahadev, 84 Khamba, and enjoy the Yamuna Aarti at the Ghat. Overnight stay at Hotel.

Day 03: Barsana & Govardhan Excursion

Proceed to Barsana and visit Radha Rani Temple (Shriji Temple), Ladli Ji Temple, Nandgaon, Nand Bhavan Temple, Yashoda Kund, and Sunheri Kothi. Later, visit the sacred Govardhan Parvat. Overnight stay at Hotel.

Day 04: Agra Sightseeing & Departure

After breakfast, check out and head to Agra to visit Taj Mahal and Agra Fort. After lunch, drop at Agra Railway Station. Tour ends with beautiful memories.
